Our Story

A slice of God's Own Country, in Mysuru

Cloverdale Cuisine began with a simple idea: bring the unhurried, coconut-scented cooking of Kerala's backwaters to Mysuru's table, exactly as it's made back home — slow-roasted spices, banana-leaf service, and recipes passed down rather than written down.

Every curry starts with fresh coconut ground that morning, our fish comes in before sunrise, and our kitchen runs on the same hygiene and hospitality standards we'd want for our own family — because most days, it is our family cooking it.
